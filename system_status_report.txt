3. FILTER THRESHOLDS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Aggregate risk pass threshold   : ≤ 75
  Authority risk — freeze active  : +45 pts
  Authority risk — mint active    : +35 pts
  Creator risk — blacklisted      : 95 (auto-reject)
  Concentration — creator > 90%   : 95 (critical)

  Decision thresholds:
    SIGNAL_EARLY  : score ≥ 70  AND  confidence ≥ 0.65  AND  agg_risk ≤ 45
    MONITOR       : score ≥ 50  AND  confidence ≥ 0.45  AND  agg_risk ≤ 65
    WARN          : score ≥ 30
    REJECT        : score < 30
